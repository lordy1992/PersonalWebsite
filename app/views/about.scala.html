About

A brief biography

I am Jeremy Lord, your host and humble narrator, and a software developer from Vancouver, BC. This is a website that I created, first as a personal project, but now as a repository for my scattered thoughts. This is where I will catalog my writing on software development, math, fiction, and any other topic that I find interesting.

I currently work at Amazon as a software development engineer, and I previously worked as an intern at Broadcom. In addition to my job in software, I am an aspiring (unpublished) writer, which is why this website doubles as a hosting platform for fiction that I have written.
