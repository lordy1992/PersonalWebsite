Jeremy's Musings

Technical blog, fiction, rants, whatever strikes my fancy

This website began as an exercise in learning Scala and the Play Framework, but I have since decided to use it as my personal website (read: soapbox) for both technical rants and fiction. Perhaps even the occasional musing on life, the universe and everything.

I am a software developer and aspiring writer, and on this website you will find content devoted to both interests. The site contains a technical blog that I will update when inspiration hits. It will eventually contain tutorials, notes on personal projects, and meandering articles on technical topics within the umbrella of computer science and engineering. On the non-technical front, the 'Writing' section of the site contains a page with a selection of writing samples that I have decided to showcase.

Feel free to send me a message on the 'Contact' page if you have any comments or questions.
